About

What the application is about

The CamelCalculator is a project to learn from and to show other people how to create iOS applications using SwiftUI.

The application uses different aspects of the body of your friends and computes a number of fictional camels that your friends might be worth. The algorithm is completely created by my imagination and does not resemble any real values.

Feel free to use the sources and resources to realize your own iOS projects.

Disclaimer

Please consider this app as a joke. There is no serious situation in which other persons, their life or their actions may be counted in camels or any other currency.

Data Privacy

The application Camel Calculator does not collect any personal information. All data you enter will only be stored on your phone. The application does not send data somewhere else.
